NTU VIDA Lab

We are the Visualization and Intelligent Data Analytics Lab (VIDA Lab) at Nanyang Technological University (NTU). Our mission is to advance the frontiers of data visualization, human-computer interaction and human-centered artificial intelligence, with the aim of augmenting human’s ability to understand and analyze large-scale data in various domains (e.g., FinTech, quantum computing and education) as well as AI models.
